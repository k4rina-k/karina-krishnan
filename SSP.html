Summer Science Program

Astrophysics

The Summer Science Program (SSP) is a highly selective (~15% acceptance rate; 588/3700+ applications internationally) residential research program affiliated with MIT, Caltech, and Harvey Mudd. 36 high school students from around the world come together to collaborate on a real-world research project. I participated in the Astrophysics program at Georgia College and State University.
